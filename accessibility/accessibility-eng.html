Content Manager and Technical Operator Juraj Ondovčík is committed to ensuring the accessibility of this website in accordance with Act No. 95/2019 Coll. on Information Technologies in Public Administration and the relevant implementing regulations (Decree No. 78/2020 Coll. and its amendments) and in compliance with the requirements of international standards WCAG 2.1 level AA.

Compliance Status

This website is fully compliant with the requirements. During a recent redesign, we implemented comprehensive accessibility measures – from semantic markup of elements to sufficient contrast, to the possibility of full navigation for assistive technologies.

Preparation of this Statement

This statement was prepared in 2026.

The evaluation of the website's compliance with accessibility requirements was carried out with reasonable consideration by an expert and automatic evaluation of compliance with standards and rules.
